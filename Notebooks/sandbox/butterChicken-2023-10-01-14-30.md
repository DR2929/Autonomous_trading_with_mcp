# Butter Chicken Recipe

## Ingredients:
- 1 lb (450 g) boneless chicken, cut into bite-sized pieces
- 1 cup plain yogurt
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice
- 1 tablespoon garam masala
- 1 tablespoon ground cumin
- 1/2 tablespoon turmeric
- 1/2 tablespoon chili powder
- 4 tablespoons butter
- 1 onion, finely chopped
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tablespoon ginger, minced
- 1 can (15 oz) tomato puree
- 1 cup heavy cream
- Salt to taste
- Fresh cilantro, for garnish

## Instructions:

1. **Marinate the Chicken**: In a bowl, combine the chicken pieces with yogurt, lemon juice, garam masala, cumin, turmeric, and chili powder. Marinate for at least 1 hour or overnight if possible.

2. **Cook the Chicken**: In a large skillet, melt 2 tablespoons of butter over medium heat. Add the marinated chicken (discard any excess marinade) and cook for about 5-7 minutes or until browned. Remove chicken and set aside.

3. **Prepare the Sauce**: In the same skillet, add the remaining butter and saut√© onions until translucent. Add minced garlic and ginger, and cook for an additional minute. Stir in the tomato puree, and let it simmer for 10 minutes.

4. **Combine**: Return the chicken to the skillet. Stir in the heavy cream, and let it cook on low heat for another 10 minutes. Season with salt to taste.

5. **Serve**: Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve hot with naan or rice.

## Enjoy your delicious Butter Chicken!
