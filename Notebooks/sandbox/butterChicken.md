# Butter Chicken Recipe

## Ingredients
- 500g chicken, cut into bite-sized pieces
- 1 cup plain yogurt
- 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
- 1 tablespoon red chili powder
- 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
- 1 teaspoon garam masala
- Salt, to taste
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 1 medium onion, finely chopped
- 1 can (400g) crushed tomatoes
- 1 cup heavy cream
- Fresh cilantro, for garnish

## Instructions
1. In a bowl, combine chicken, yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric, garam masala, and salt. Marinate for at least 1 hour or overnight for best results.
2. Heat butter in a pan over medium heat. Add chopped onions and saut√© until translucent.
3. Add marinated chicken to the pan and cook until browned.
4. Stir in crushed tomatoes and simmer for 15-20 minutes.
5. Add heavy cream and cook for an additional 5-10 minutes.
6. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve with rice or naan.
